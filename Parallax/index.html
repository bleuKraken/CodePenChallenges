Parallax and Fixed Images

What is parallax scrolling? Parallax scrolling is a technique where a background image can move at a slower pace than what is in the foreground. It is often described as a 3D effect where an image is under or behind the content and becomes noticable when a user is scrolling.

Fixed Images

Fixed images are images that are placed with a defined area and do not move. Fixed images with some css can turn into something similar to parallax.

Combining fixed images and parallax can both be used to create some pretty interesting background with images!
